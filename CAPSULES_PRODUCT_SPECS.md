## Freshness & Shelf Life

### The Challenge

Ground coffee loses freshness much faster than whole beans:
- Whole beans: 4-6 weeks optimal, 2-3 months acceptable
- Ground coffee: 1-2 weeks optimal, 4-6 weeks acceptable
- Capsules (sealed): 2-4 weeks optimal, 6-8 weeks acceptable

### Freshness Strategy

```
PRODUCTION SCHEDULE:
‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Roast weekly (every Monday)
‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Grind + fill Tuesday-Wednesday
‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Ship Thursday-Friday
‚îî‚îÄ‚îÄ Customer receives within 3-5 days of roasting

BATCH SIZING:
‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Produce based on orders + modest buffer
‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Never hold filled capsules >2 weeks
‚îî‚îÄ‚îÄ "Roasted on" date on every pack
```

### Packaging for Freshness

| Method | Cost | Freshness Extension | Recommendation |
|--------|------|---------------------|----------------|

**Recommendation:** Nitrogen flush bags for standard packs, valve bags for larger packs.
